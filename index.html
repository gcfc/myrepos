About me

Hello! I am George Chen, a mechanical engineering undergrad studying in Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

My years in the institute have made me a fast learner, both in the academic field and in the industry. Once a subject sparks a tiny bit of my interest, I get carried away unraveling its mysteries, one after another. Hence I have a versatile set of skills and a wide range of interests, including controls, automation, thermal engineering, and design. While the courses I took in the institute gave me a strong base of theoretical knowledge, my previous research, collaborative projects, and industry experience allowed me to apply said knowledge into practical engineering. I am very fortunate to have collaborated with many passionate people on innovative and impactful projects, and I continue to seek for such opportunities.
